# Product Brief

SafetyMap Intelligence is a country intelligence dashboard for quickly understanding safety and stability context across the world.

## Problem

Travelers, analysts, operators, and globally distributed teams often need a fast answer to "what is happening there now?" Existing sources are fragmented across news, advisories, social feeds, datasets, and internal notes.

## Product Goal

Create a map-first dashboard that helps users scan countries, compare risk context, and inspect recent incidents through a clear, practical interface.

## Initial Users

- Individual travelers doing high-level destination checks.
- Security-aware teams monitoring countries of interest.
- Analysts prototyping country intelligence workflows.

## MVP Outcome

The first milestone should prove the dashboard experience, not the data pipeline. It will use mock country intelligence data to validate layout, navigation, map interaction, country search, country side panels, and timeline presentation.

## Non-Goals For The First Visual Milestone

- No authentication.
- No real ingestion.
- No AI summaries or scoring automation.
- No database.
- No backend API.
- No production deployment.
